What does a Pro subscription unlock?

A Pro subscription unlocks Cloud Save & Sync, Version History (up to 10 versions per file), AI tools (monthly AI actions), and an expanded Recent list (up to 100 with pins and search). Your subscription remains active until the end of your paid period if you cancel.

Privacy by default

Open Excel processes files locally in your browser. Cloud features upload files to your Open Excel account storage, and AI features send only the selected cells/content needed to generate results.

FAQ

Will taxes be added?

Depending on your location, VAT or sales tax may apply and is calculated during checkout.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime — your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do refunds work?

Refunds are handled by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. You can request help or a refund using the support link in your Paddle receipt email or via Paddle order support.

What happens if I exceed limits?

You’ll be prompted to upgrade storage or wait until your monthly AI actions reset, depending on the limit.
