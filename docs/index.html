Key features

FREE Excel viewer & editor

Open .xls and .xlsx files directly in Chrome: no Excel app required.

Edit in Google Sheets

Upload the current workbook to your Drive and continue editing online.

Multi-sheet navigation

Switch between tabs in one click and keep working fluidly.

Save as PDF

Export the active sheet to a printer-friendly PDF in seconds.

5-step workflow

1. Pin the toolbar icon after installation.
2. Click the “Open Excel” icon.
3. Choose or drag a .xls/.xlsx file.
4. Browse sheets, zoom, or export to PDF.
5. Hit “Edit in Google Sheets” to upload and keep editing.

Who is it for?

Students, accountants, managers: anyone who needs quick spreadsheet access without app.
